Project Overview

This project delivers a consulting-style competitive intelligence study of three leading asset managers: BlackRock, State Street and Invesco. It combines fundamental analysis of 2024 10-K / annual report disclosures with structured financial comparison, technology and platform assessment, and a forward-looking strategic view.

Scope

  • Financial performance & profitability
  • Business model & client mix
  • Product & AUM composition
  • Technology & platforms (Aladdin, Alpha, etc.)
  • Risk & regulatory environment

Deliverables

  • Full competitive intelligence report (PDF)
  • Executive summary (2 pages)
  • Interactive Streamlit dashboard
  • GitHub repo with code and documentation

Tools & Skills

  • Python (pandas, plotly, Streamlit)
  • Financial & strategic analysis
  • Data storytelling & visualization
  • Working with SEC / regulatory filings

Selected Insights

BlackRock

Combines unmatched scale (> $11.5T AUM) with the Aladdin platform to create a powerful scale + technology moat, while pushing aggressively into private markets and data.

State Street

A custody-centric model built on $46T+ AUC/A and State Street Alpha; earnings are more tied to servicing fees and NII than traditional asset managers.

Invesco

Competes as a large independent manager with a strong ETF and QQQ franchise, but faces scale and fee-pressure challenges versus mega-managers.
